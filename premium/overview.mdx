Luster Premium is a subscription that unlocks a set of powerful features beyond what the free tier provides. Premium commands are visible to everyone in your server, but only active subscribers can run them. Features are organized into three tiers — each tier includes everything from the tier below it, so higher tiers get the full feature set.

## Check your premium status

Run `,check-premium` in any channel where Luster is present. The bot will reply with your current tier and a list of commands available to you. If you don't have premium, the response will tell you so and show you what you're missing.

```
,check-premium
```

## Tier structure

Luster Premium has three tiers. Each tier builds on the one before it.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tier 1">
    The entry-level premium tier. Tier 1 gives you access to server engagement and moderation utility commands including role comparison, the server pet, reaction tracking, and emoji usage stats. Bot profile customization commands are also included, though those are restricted to the bot owner.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tier 2">
    Everything in Tier 1, plus server administration tools. Tier 2 adds channel permission sync, which lets you audit and bulk-fix channels that are out of sync with their category permissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tier 3">
    Everything in Tier 1 and Tier 2, plus the full AI suite. Tier 3 adds AI-powered text chat, image analysis, OCR text extraction, and vision queries — all powered by local AI models.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to get premium

Join the official Luster Discord server to purchase a subscription or ask about available plans.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Luster server">
    Visit [discord.gg/vBYqNsZ2Kx](https://discord.gg/vBYqNsZ2Kx) to join the official support and community server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the premium channel">
    Look for the premium or subscriptions channel in the server for current pricing and purchase options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your access">
    Once your subscription is active, run `,check-premium` to confirm your tier and see your available commands.
  </Step>
</Steps>
