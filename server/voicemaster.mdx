VoiceMaster gives your members the ability to create personal, temporary voice channels on demand. When someone joins a designated **Create** channel, Luster spawns a new voice channel named after them and moves them into it. They get full control over that channel through a persistent control panel. When the last person leaves, Luster deletes the channel automatically — no manual cleanup needed.

## Setting up VoiceMaster

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run the setup command">
    ```
    ,voicemaster setup
    ```

    Luster creates everything it needs in one go:

    - A **luster** category
    - A **#panel** text channel (read-only for members) containing the control panel
    - A **Create** voice channel — joining this triggers a new temporary VC
  </Step>
  <Step title="Point members to the Create channel">
    Tell your members to join the **Create** voice channel whenever they want a temporary channel. Luster moves them to their own channel immediately after.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need the **Administrator** permission to run `,voicemaster setup`. The alias `,vm setup` works as a shorthand.
</Note>

## Member controls

Once a member is in their temporary voice channel, they can use the buttons on the **Voice Master Control Panel** in the `#panel` channel. Only the channel's owner can use these controls.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lock and unlock">
    **Lock** prevents anyone from joining the channel. **Unlock** re-opens it to everyone.

    Members who are already inside the channel are not affected when you lock it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ghost and unghost">
    **Ghost** hides the channel from the channel list for everyone who is not already in it. **Unghost** makes it visible again.

    Use ghost to create a hidden session without blocking joins outright — or combine ghost and lock for a fully private channel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Rename">
    Clicking **Rename** opens a modal where you can type a new name for your channel. The change takes effect immediately.

    <Tip>
      Discord rate-limits channel renames to 2 per 10 minutes. Avoid renaming repeatedly in quick succession.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Mute and unmute a user">
    **Mute** opens a user picker. Select someone in the channel to prevent them from speaking. **Unmute** restores their speak permission.

    This only affects speak permission in your channel — it does not server-mute the user.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Info">
    Shows when your channel was created, how old it is (in hours), and how many unique members have joined it during the session.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Delete">
    Permanently deletes your temporary channel. Everyone inside is disconnected immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Automatic cleanup

Luster monitors voice state changes. When the last member leaves a temporary channel, Luster deletes it without any action needed from you or your staff.

## Resetting VoiceMaster

To remove VoiceMaster entirely — including all active temporary channels, the panel channel, the Create channel, and the category — run:

```
,voicemaster reset
```

<Warning>
  `,voicemaster reset` (or `,vm reset`) deletes all channels Luster created during setup, including any currently active temporary voice channels. Members inside those channels will be disconnected.
</Warning>

## Command reference

| Command | Alias | Permission | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| `,voicemaster setup` | `,vm setup` | Administrator | Create the VoiceMaster category, panel, and Create channel. |
| `,voicemaster reset` | `,vm reset` | Administrator | Delete all VoiceMaster channels and wipe the configuration. |
