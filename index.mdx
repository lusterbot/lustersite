## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite Luster to your server">
    Use the official invite link to add Luster to your Discord server. Grant it the permissions it requests so all features work correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your prefix">
    Luster's default prefix is `,`. Change it any time with `,prefix set <new_prefix>`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure core features">
    Set up moderation roles, welcome messages, and any other features your server needs using the commands in each section of these docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore and enjoy">
    Browse the documentation to discover everything Luster can do — from AntiNuke protection to economy systems and Last.fm music tracking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Luster can do

Luster is organized into focused feature areas so you can enable exactly what your server needs:

- **Moderation** — Ban, kick, mute, timeout, hardban, force nickname, lock/hide channels, and more
- **AntiNuke** — Automatically detect and reverse mass destructive actions with configurable thresholds
- **AutoMod** — Filter blocked words and Discord invites automatically
- **Welcome & Goodbye** — Fully customizable join/leave messages with variables
- **Tickets** — Managed support ticket channels with staff controls
- **VoiceMaster** — Let members create and control their own temporary voice channels
- **Leveling** — XP-based rank system with role rewards and leaderboards
- **Economy** — Currency, jobs, gambling, trading, and a full shop system
- **Last.fm** — Connect your account and share what you're listening to
- **Giveaways** — Timed giveaways with winner selection and reroll support
- **Starboard** — Highlight the best messages in a dedicated channel
- **Premium** — AI chat, vision, OCR, and more for premium subscribers
